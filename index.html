Mocumentary "SURVIVAL"

Created by Iryna Shyshko, 2024

SUMMARY

Films about nature and animal world hold a strong position in popularity today. Sophisticated filming technology granted the humanity access to previously unthought of being seen corners of our planet. All live creatures, including human being, are finding their way to survive on Earth. We are prone to make strong statements about our will to save the planet AND ourselves from extinction. But do we really translate such statements into reality? My mocumentary is about such contradiction. Narrated with the help of AI by David Attenborough, the film poses a question - 'Where are we heading?".
